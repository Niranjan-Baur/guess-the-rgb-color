Notes :

How to play the RGB Guesing Game?

  1. There are three modes are Present ( Easy , Hard and Super Hard ). By default Super hard is selected. But you can choose any desire mode.

  2. Now, look at the RGB() number at the top.

  3. Then try to guess the actual color according to the RGB number.

  4. After winning the game a "Correct !!" message will shown in between New colors ( or Play Again ) and Easy button.
Functionality of buttons :
  1. Easy : In the easy mode, there are three colors. Choose any one of those according to the number of RGB().

  2. Hard : In the hard mode, there are six colors. Choose any one of those according to the number of RGB().

  3. Super Hard : In the Super Hard mode, there are nine colors. Choose any one of those according to the number of RGB().

  4. New colors : To click this button you can change the colors which are already sets.

  5. Play Again? : When you are win the game, the "New colors" button will change into "Play Again?".
